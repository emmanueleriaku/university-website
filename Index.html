We are a school that provides students with a comprehensive education that covers all aspects of the animation process, from storyboarding and character design to 3D modeling and special effects. We have knowledgeable and experienced instructors who are able to teach the latest software and techniques while also encouraging creativity and originality. We offer students access to the latest tools and technology, including high-end computers, software, and other equipment. We also provide a collaborative and supportive learning environment that allows students to work together and learn from one another. Additionally, we provide students with opportunities to showcase their work and build their portfolios, such as through student showcases, competitions, or internships. Ultimately, we prepare students for successful careers in the animation industry and provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to bring their creative visions to life.
